Ruda Zhang

Postdoctoral Fellow

University of Southern California

I study how human behavior affects infrastructure performance and how technology can change human behavior for better societal outcomes. Specifically, I connect strategic decision-making to urban-scale dynamics, and use engineering tools to shape incentive structure to improve social welfare. I am particularly interested in solving traffic congestion.

My research program can be framed as human-centered technology-enabled urban systems, which invovles: (1) discovery, design, and implementation of microscopic mechanisms of urban dynamics; (2) systems modeling with machine learning; (3) novel data sources and computing techniques.
